About WayKON Capital

WayKON Capital is an early-stage holding company focused on the creative industries. We partner with visionary founders and creative teams to build enduring businesses.

From fashion brands redefining style to music platforms amplifying voices—we invest in the creators and innovators driving cultural change, with a long-term vision for growth across the creative sector.

Based between London and Lagos, our approach combines creative expertise with strategic support to unlock growth and long-term value.
