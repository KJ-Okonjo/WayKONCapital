Our Services

Strategic support for creative businesses at every stage of growth

How We Add Value

We go beyond capital. Our hands-on approach combines strategic guidance, operational expertise, and creative insights to help our portfolio companies scale sustainably and build enduring brands.

01

Strategic Advisory

We work alongside founders and management teams to define vision, refine strategy, and navigate critical business decisions in the creative industries.

  • Business Model Innovation Evolving revenue models for fashion, music, and media businesses in the digital age
  • Market Positioning & Brand Strategy Defining unique market positions and building distinctive creative brands
  • Partnership Development Connecting portfolio companies with strategic partners, distributors, and collaborators
  • Capital Structure & Financing Optimizing funding strategies and connecting founders with follow-on investors
02

Operational Excellence

We help creative businesses build robust operations, optimize workflows, and implement systems that enable sustainable growth without compromising artistic integrity.

  • Talent Acquisition & Team Building Recruiting top creative and operational talent across design, production, and management
  • Supply Chain & Production Management Optimizing manufacturing, distribution, and fulfillment for fashion and goods businesses
  • Technology & Systems Implementation Deploying creative tools, e-commerce platforms, and content management systems
  • Financial Planning & Management Building financial infrastructure, budgeting processes, and performance tracking
03

Growth & Scaling

We support portfolio companies in expanding their reach, entering new markets, and scaling creatively and commercially while maintaining brand authenticity.

  • Audience Development & Marketing Building communities, growing audiences, and developing effective marketing campaigns
  • Channel Expansion & Distribution Launching new channels, entering retail, expanding digital presence, and international growth
  • Product & Content Development Developing new collections, content formats, product lines, and creative offerings
  • M&A and Strategic Partnerships Identifying acquisition targets, merger opportunities, and strategic collaborations
