Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WayKON Capital

General Questions

WayKON Capital is a holding company focused on the creative industries. We invest in, acquire, and build businesses across fashion, music, art, advertising, and media. Our approach combines capital, strategic guidance, operational support, and industry networks to help creative businesses scale sustainably.

We specialize exclusively in creative industries, bringing deep domain expertise that most generalist investors lack. We're hands-on partners, not passive check-writers. Our team has built and scaled creative businesses ourselves, and we understand the unique balance between artistic vision and commercial success.

We invest across stages—from seed to growth—and also acquire businesses. Typically, we look for companies with proven product-market fit, early revenue traction, and strong founding teams. For acquisitions, we target profitable businesses with $5M+ in annual revenue.

Investment Criteria

We prioritize: (1) Strong founding teams with creative and commercial acumen, (2) Differentiated products, brands, or content with clear market positioning, (3) Business models with scalable unit economics, (4) Alignment with our focus areas in creative industries, and (5) Opportunity for us to add meaningful strategic and operational value.

Yes. While we're based primarily in North America and Europe, we invest globally. We have portfolio companies in six countries and actively seek opportunities in emerging creative markets.

Our initial investments typically range from $2M to $15M, depending on stage and capital requirements. We reserve capital for follow-on investments and can lead or participate in larger rounds through our network of co-investors.

Portfolio Support

We provide hands-on support across strategy, operations, and growth. This includes: strategic advisory and board participation, access to our network of creatives, operators, and industry leaders, operational support (finance, HR, systems, supply chain), marketing and audience development guidance, and introductions to follow-on investors and strategic partners.

It depends on the investment. For majority investments and acquisitions, we typically take board seats. For minority investments, we may take a board seat or observer role depending on the investment size and our level of involvement.

We're as involved as founders want us to be. Some companies prefer strategic guidance and network access; others want deeper operational support. We tailor our engagement to each company's needs, but we always respect founder autonomy and creative vision.

Application Process

Start by reaching out through our contact form or via email at info@waykoncapital.com. Include a brief overview of your business, team, traction, and what you're raising. If there's a fit, we'll schedule an initial conversation and request a pitch deck.

From initial conversation to term sheet typically takes 4-6 weeks. Due diligence and closing add another 4-8 weeks, depending on complexity. We move quickly for the right opportunities and aim to be founder-friendly throughout the process.

Generally, we do not sign NDAs for initial pitches, which is standard practice in venture capital. Once we move into detailed due diligence and term discussions, we're happy to execute mutual confidentiality agreements.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 1-2 business days.
