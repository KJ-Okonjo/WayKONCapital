Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our investment approach and services

Investment Approach

We focus on growth-stage companies across technology, healthcare, infrastructure, manufacturing, real estate, and consumer goods sectors. Our ideal investments are businesses with strong management teams, proven business models, and clear paths to scaled growth. We typically invest in companies generating between $10M-$100M in annual revenue.

Our investments typically range from $25M to $200M, with the flexibility to invest more for exceptional opportunities. We can participate in minority or majority positions depending on the situation and management preferences.

We take a long-term view with our investments, typically holding positions for 5-10 years. Unlike traditional private equity firms with fixed fund cycles, our evergreen capital structure allows us to focus on sustainable value creation rather than forced exit timelines.

Yes. While we have a strong presence in North America and Europe, we actively invest in high-growth markets worldwide. We have portfolio companies operating across 8 countries and continue to expand our geographic footprint.

Partnership & Services

We offer comprehensive support including strategic advisory, operational management assistance, financial planning, M&A support, talent recruitment, and growth strategy development. Our hands-on approach means we work closely with management teams to implement initiatives and drive results.

Our level of involvement varies based on company needs and management preferences. We typically maintain board seats and provide strategic guidance, but we respect management autonomy in daily operations. For companies requiring more hands-on support, we can embed team members to assist with specific functions.

In select cases, yes. We occasionally provide advisory and consulting services to non-portfolio companies, particularly when there's potential for a future investment relationship or strategic partnership. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Process & Timeline

Our process typically includes: (1) Initial meeting and preliminary review, (2) Detailed due diligence including financial, operational, and market analysis, (3) Investment committee review, (4) Term sheet negotiation, (5) Final due diligence and legal documentation, (6) Closing. We strive to move efficiently while conducting thorough analysis.

From initial contact to closing typically takes 8-12 weeks, though timelines can vary based on deal complexity and due diligence requirements. We can move faster for the right opportunities and work to accommodate company timelines where possible.

The best approach is to submit an executive summary and investment materials through our contact form or email us directly at partnerships@waykoncapital.com. Include key information about your business, market opportunity, financial performance, and funding requirements. We review all submissions and respond within 5-7 business days.

About WayKON Capital

WayKON Capital is led by a team of experienced investors and operators with backgrounds at leading investment firms and Fortune 500 companies. Our team brings expertise across multiple industries and functional areas. You can learn more about our leadership on our About page.

Our headquarters is located in New York City, with additional team members and resources in London, San Francisco, and Singapore. This global presence allows us to support portfolio companies across multiple time zones and geographies.

WayKON Capital operates with permanent capital, which means we don't operate on traditional fund cycles. For qualified institutional and accredited investors interested in co-investment opportunities, please contact us directly.
